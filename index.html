About Us

"At St. Francis Church, we are dedicated to fostering a vibrant and inclusive community centered around faith, love, and service. For over five decades, our church has been a beacon of hope and spiritual guidance in the heart of our city. Rooted in the teachings of Christ, we strive to create an environment where individuals from all walks of life feel welcomed, valued, and supported. Our mission is not only to nurture the spiritual growth of our members but also to actively engage with and uplift our broader community through outreach programs, charitable initiatives, and acts of compassion. Together, we embrace the journey of faith, seeking to embody the values of love, kindness, and unity in all that we do."
